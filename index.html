Mapping Citi Bikes ridership in September 2020, September 2021

By Richard A. Abbey | June 13, 2022


The CitiBike provides comprehensive data on the usage of its bike share services across New York City. The published data details rides' origins and destinations. The anonymized data also provides details on users such as their birth dates, subscription types etc. In this particular project, data for September 2020 and September 2021 were processed for visualization.

The visualization made use of data on the station names where the trips originated as well as where the trips ended. The location of these stations, provided in the data, was converted into GEOJSON using geopandas library in python. The resultant map illustrates the popularity of a each Citi Bike Station. A bigger circle indicates that the station had more trips either originating from it or terminating at it.
